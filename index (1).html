General Medicine

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic illnesses. Routine check-ups, health screenings, and long-term wellness management.

Paediatrics

Specialised care for infants, children, and adolescents. Growth monitoring, vaccinations, and management of childhood illnesses.

Gynaecology

Women's health including prenatal care, reproductive health consultations, and gynaecological examinations in a safe, private environment.

Cardiology Consultation

ECG, blood pressure management, cholesterol screening, and expert consultation for cardiovascular risk assessment and prevention.

Diabetes Management

Holistic diabetes care — blood glucose monitoring, diet counselling, medication management, and complication prevention strategies.

Preventive Health

Annual wellness exams, vaccination schedules, cancer screenings, and lifestyle guidance to keep you healthy before illness strikes.

Vaccination Services

Complete immunisation for all age groups — childhood vaccines, travel vaccinations, booster doses, and flu shots throughout the year.

Minor Procedures

Wound care, suturing, abscess drainage, removal of minor skin lesions, and other outpatient procedures performed in a sterile environment.

Diagnostic Lab Work

On-site sample collection for blood tests, urinalysis, and pathology. Fast-tracked results with doctor interpretation and follow-up care.

About Brinda Clinic

A Clinic Built on Trust & Care

Founded with a singular vision — to bring world-class medical care closer to the people of the Andaman & Nicobar Islands — Brinda Clinic has served this community for over 15 years with unwavering commitment.

We combine modern diagnostic technology with a deeply personal approach to medicine. Every patient who visits us is treated with the same dignity, attention, and respect, regardless of their condition or background.

Certified Practitioners

All our doctors hold recognised national certifications and pursue continuous medical education.

Modern Equipment

State-of-the-art diagnostic tools ensure accurate results and faster treatment decisions.

Patient-First Culture

From reception to consultation, every interaction is shaped by empathy and respect.

Confidential & Safe

Your health data and privacy are protected under strict medical ethics and digital security.
